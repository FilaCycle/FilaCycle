About the Project

FilaCycle is my STEM MAD 2025 project, focused on transforming used PET bottles into usable 3D printing filament. Using a cutter and homemade pullstruder, I recycle bottles and use them to print strong, useful, and fun objects.

How It Works

  1. Collect PET bottles (soft drink bottles)
  2. Cut into strips with a custom bottle cutter
  3. Pull into filament using a heated pullstruder
  4. 3D print toys, tools, and more!
